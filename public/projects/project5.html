THE O is an app that works combined with THE O device. It connects the device with the phone and aso checks if the device is with you or if you forgot/lost it. In that case the app alets you.

I worked together with the development team to create an interface that shows in a simple way all the different states of the devices: Lost, save, with you... as well as display crearly what device is attached to each of your belongings.

After months of development and countless iterations the app is available in both the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store.
