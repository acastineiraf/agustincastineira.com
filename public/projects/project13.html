Sometimes I participate in Design Contests. This one was a Web Design contest were the main focus was simplicuty and clear design.

I started with simple mockups getting inspired from the whatsapp website or some mobile apps' corporative websites and then I iterated on that design to create 3 different options:

  • Hipster/millennial target group
  • Young/fancy target group
  • Minimal design target group

The final resilt is simple yet very attractive for each of the considered target users.
