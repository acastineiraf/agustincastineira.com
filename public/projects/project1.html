Working in THE O team I focused on consistency for all the different public faces of the company. Minimalistic design, fashion look, a carefree lifestyle and wearable tech were some of the basic concepts we worked around to generate the brand identity through all of our product components: Web, App, Product and Packaging.

The process we normally followed was:

  • Brief definition of the requirements.
  • First quick iteration to get a couple or three ideas to put on the table.
  • Iterations of feedback + discussion + conclusion + next steps + repeat.
  • Final deliverables.

We defined deadlines to stop the iterations process soon enough to prepare the final deliverables on time and finally we managed to get the product on the market!
