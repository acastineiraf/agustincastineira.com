This is a personal side project I worked on for a couple of weeks. The idea is simple, each dot means a different week, from your birth to your 80's. And the colours means the following:

birthday week | week you already lived | week of the future

The main idea I want you to get from this poster is to live life, be espontaneous and enjoy the moment. Decide what to do each week of your life, because time goes by!
